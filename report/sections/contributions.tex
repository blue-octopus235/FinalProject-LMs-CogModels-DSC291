\section*{Author Contributions}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Jackson Wilke} built the end-to-end LSTM training and evaluation
    infrastructure (shared vocabulary, leak-free held-out split, minimal-pair
    scoring, attractor-gap metric, result visualizations, statistical tests) and
    the RNNG train+eval pipeline (\texttt{run\_rnng\_datahub.py}), including
    running all five RNNG seed-1 conditions.
    Wrote the Methods, Experimental Setup, and Appendix sections.
  \item \textbf{Kelly Fu} built the noise-injection pipeline that produced the
    five corrupted training corpora and validated the injections.
    Wrote the Introduction, Background \& Related Work, Abstract, and Conclusion
    sections.
  \item \textbf{Sanjana Garimella} added mid-training checkpointing and the
    trajectory evaluation script, ran the full three-seed LSTM sweep and
    learning-trajectory sweep on Colab, and expanded the literature review.
    Wrote the Results and Discussion sections.
\end{itemize}
