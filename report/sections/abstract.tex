Can language models acquire grammatical rules from noisy linguistic input, and does structural inductive bias improve robustness to such noise?

To anwser these questions, we systematically inject subject--verb agreement (SVA) at rates ranging from 0% to 50% to a large corpus of wiki-English sentences, creating training corpora with controlled levels of grammatical corruption
